The Host

Yeukai Kajidori

Yeukai Kajidori is a health and social care leader with over 20 years of experience, an MBA, Level 7 qualifications in Health and Social Care and Strategic Management, and a CQC Registered Healthcare Operations Manager.

From 2016 to 2024, he hosted the Yeukai Business Show — interviewing over 550 global business leaders, entrepreneurs, and innovators. The show reached iTunes Rank #3 and was featured by BBC, ITV, Sky, Bloomberg, and CBS Radio.

Today, Yeukai leads The Kajidori Collective — a specialist training, consulting, and mentoring practice for health and social care organisations across the UK.

"Every great leader I interviewed had one thing in common: they never stopped learning. This show exists to share those lessons with the world."
